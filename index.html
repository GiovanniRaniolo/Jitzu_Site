About


 Music producer, sound designer and and dj from Sicily, Jitzu has been shaping the music scene since 2002. His unique blend of electronic genres is set to gain even more popularity in the years ahead.


 Jitzu began playing the piano at six and later mastered various instruments, including the electric guitar, bass, and drums. His passion for music led him to study Musicology in Bologna, focusing on experimentation in audio technology and the rise of electronic music. In 2006, the debuted with Autist Records in Berlin, marking the start of his production career.


 Not just a producer, Jitzu is also a dynamic dj and live performer, known for his engaging sets. His music has graced events and festivals, earning him a loyal following. As an innovator, Jitzu explores new horizons in music creation and distribution. His recent project, 0x-Jitzu, delves into blockchain technology and NFTs, merging futuristic aesthetics with his signature sound.
